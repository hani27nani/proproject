Year 1

My first year learning how to code was a truly eye-opening and formative experience. I was introduced to the fundamentals of computer programming, gaining a solid understanding of core concepts such as basic functions, logical operations, and the structure of various coding languages. This year laid the groundwork for my journey in technology, as I explored programming languages like JavaScript and learned important concepts such as binary code and simple calculations. I’m especially grateful to Ms. Hart, who played a vital role in supporting and guiding me through the learning process. Her encouragement and teaching helped me build confidence in my skills. Year 2

My second year in programming was filled with growth and exciting new opportunities.
